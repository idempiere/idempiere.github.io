# Lead

<span className="badge badge--primary">Window</span> <span className="badge badge--secondary">ID 53153</span>

*25/08/2013 → 04/01/2021*

![Lead](./img/lead.png)

**Description:** Maintain Leads

**Comment/Help:** The Lead Window allows you to maintain Leads (can be Sales or Purchase). These are unqualified contacts to who you wish to recruit or market to.
