| Active | The record is active in the system | There are two methods of making records unavailable in the system: One is to delete the record, the other is to de-activate the record. A de-activated record is not available for selection, but available for reports.<br/>There are two reasons for de-activating and not deleting records:<br/>(1) The system requires the record for audit purposes.<br/>(2) The record is referenced by other records. E.g., you cannot delete a Business Partner, if there are invoices for this partner record existing.
