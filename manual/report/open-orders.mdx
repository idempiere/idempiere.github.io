# Open Orders

![Open Orders](./img/open-orders.png)

**Description:** Open Order Report

**Comment/Help:** Orders with quantities to deliver (Backordered) or quantities to invoice

| **Name** | **Description** | **Comment/Help** | **Technical Data** |
