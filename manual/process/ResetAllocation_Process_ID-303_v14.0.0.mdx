# Reset Allocation

<span className="badge badge--primary">Process</span> <span className="badge badge--secondary">ID 303</span>

*15/08/2004 → 21/02/2005*

![Reset Allocation](/img/manual/ResetAllocation-Process_iDempiere_v14.0.0.png)

**Description:** Reset (delete) allocation of invoices to payments

**Comment/Help:** Delete Allocations for a business partner or business partner group, accounting date range or individual allocation. In contrast to "Void" in "View Allocation", the allocation is deleted (no trace), if the period is open.

**Classname:** `org.compiere.process.AllocationReset`
