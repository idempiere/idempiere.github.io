# Workflow to Tenant

<span className="badge badge--primary">Process</span> <span className="badge badge--secondary">ID 309</span>

*28/09/2004 → 10/03/2022*

![Workflow to Tenant](./img/workflow-to-tenant.png)

**Description:** Move custom workflow elements to the current tenant

**Comment/Help:** The security architecture prevents you from adding tenant specific modifications/customizations to existing standard workflows.  This process moves your customization made on system level to this tenant to add tenant specific details.

**Classname:** `org.compiere.wf.WorkflowMoveToClient`

## Table: Process Parameters

| **Name** | **Description** | **Comment/Help** | **Technical Data** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tenant | Tenant for this installation. | A Tenant is a company or a legal entity. You cannot share data between Tenants. | AD_Client_ID<br/>Table Direct |
| Workflow | Workflow or combination of tasks | The Workflow field identifies a unique Workflow in the system. | AD_Workflow_ID<br/>Table Direct |
