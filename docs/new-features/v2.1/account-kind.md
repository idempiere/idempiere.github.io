**Goal:** Functional

**Developer:** Nicolas Micoud

**Description:**

In order to identify if an account is managing business partners (like Accounts Receivable), or managing products (like Depreciation).  No functionality added around these two new fields, can be used for validations in plugins, or for reporting purposes.
