## Enhancing statement of account report to display Business Partner and Account column

When we generate statement of account report for multiple account, the account column is use full to know GL entry is related to which GL account.
This help in generating Account Ledger having credit and debit of different accounts related to a BPartner.  

When a ledger of accounts are printed, we can see the business partner column and account column will give more insights to statement. For example printing Bank account statement or Cash statement, we can see the business partner column and account column will give more insights to statement.
