**Description:**

When you want to know how many items of a product you have on hand, you can use the Storage Per Product report. It is the only report in iDempiere that summarizes quantities. However, it previously did not provide information about quantity available, quantity reserved, and quantity ordered.

With this update, the Storage Per Product report now displays all quantities summarized per product.
