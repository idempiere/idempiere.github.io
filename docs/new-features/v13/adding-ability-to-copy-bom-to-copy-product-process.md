# Adding ability to Copy BOM to Copy Product process

**Goal:** User Experience

**Developer:** pultzlucas

**Feature** **Ticket:** [IDEMPIERE-6874](https://idempiere.atlassian.net/browse/IDEMPIERE-6874)

### Description
The **Copy Product** process, now copies the following details:
- Prices
- Substitutes
- Related Products
- Replenishment
- Business Partner Product
- BOM Formulas and BOM Lines (new in r13)
- UOM Conversions (new in r13)
- Downloads (not used in iDempiere)
