**Goal:** User Experience (Usability)

**Developer:** Hengsin ([talk](https://wiki.idempiere.org/en/User_talk:Hengsin))

**Sponsor:** [Trek Global](https://www.trekglobal.com//)

**Description:**

The goal of the ticket is to allow users to switch between grid and form view at detail pane.

**How to do it?**

1. Click the Grid Toggle button at the Detail Pane toolbar. You can also use the shortcut key of Shift+Alt+T.
